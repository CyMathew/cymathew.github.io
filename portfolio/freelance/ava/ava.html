Audio Visual Architects is a company based in California that provides setup "for all your home automation and audio-visual needs".

They requested for a conversion all their older Flash based content to HTML5 elements. This included banner and header images on every page of their site, their navigation menu, all buttons throughout the site, the site logo and tagline as well as fixing broken links

This site uses an old-style table layout, so in order to not break or have to redo the styling I was asked to keep the table layout but modify it. I was unfortunately not contracted to update or change the layout of the site for them
