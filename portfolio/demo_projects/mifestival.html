I was inspired by a Photoshop website mockup on Behance, found here

Because this mockup provided free PSD files, I was able to look at those to determine distances, colors, font sizes and other small pieces to inform my demo creation

I did have to change some details of the demo site for various reasons

  • I was not able to find the stock images for some of the sections, so I had to find suitable replacements
  • The mockup uses Helvetica Neue for subtext and links but I replaced it with a similar looking san-serif font
